Methodology Note

Data is synthetically generated to mirror the distributions observed in public fire brigade open datasets (e.g. London Fire Brigade incident records). The purpose is to demonstrate analytical capability: identifying temporal peaks, high-risk incident categories, and response time performance — the same analytical lens applied in forensic fire investigation to identify patterns, anomalies, and causal factors. In a live deployment, this pipeline would ingest real incident logs via SQL or CSV, with automated anomaly flagging for unusual clusters of electrical or explosion incidents.
